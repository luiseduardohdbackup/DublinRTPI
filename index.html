Dublin RTPI

Dublin RTPI is a mobile app that allows you to access real-time public transport information such as when is the next train to Bray arriving at Tara Station? or how many Dublin Bikes are available in the St. Stephen's Green South Station? at any time in under 3 seconds.

One app to rule them all

Dublin RTPI allows you to access all the main public transport services real-time information from one single app.

Easy, elegant & quick

Dublin RTPI has been designed to be really easy and quick to use and provides a minimalist and elegant user interface. You can get to the information that you need with as litle as three simple steps.

1. Open the Dublin RTPI app

2. Select how do you want to travel.

3. Select one of the station in the map.

4. Access the real-time information.
